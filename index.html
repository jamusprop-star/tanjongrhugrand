Tanjong Rhu Grand

Tanjong Rhu Grand (also known as Tanjong Rhu Residences) is the first Government Land Sale (GLS) private residential site in the Tanjong Rhu enclave in nearly three decades. This rare opportunity offers 525 thoughtfully crafted residential units with a fresh 99-year lease in a predominantly mature, freehold/long-leasehold waterfront enclave.

Located at Tanjong Rhu Road in District 15, this new launch offers scenic waterfront living along the Geylang River and Kallang Basin. Residents enjoy dual MRT access with both Tanjong Rhu MRT and Katong Park MRT (Thomson-East Coast Line) within a 10-minute walk, plus proximity to the Sports Hub and Kallang Alive Masterplan.

Tanjong Rhu Road Condo Highlights

Connectivity

Dual MRT access within 10-minute walk to Tanjong Rhu MRT and Katong Park MRT (Thomson-East Coast Line), offering direct connections to Marina Bay, Shenton Way, Maxwell, and Orchard Road. Immediate access to East Coast Parkway (ECP), Kallang–Paya Lebar Expressway (KPE), and Nicoll Highway.

Lifestyle

Direct access to Singapore Sports Hub, National Stadium, OCBC Aquatic Centre, and Water Sports Centre. Short drive to Kallang Wave Mall and the vibrant Katong and Joo Chiat dining enclaves. Walking distance to waterfront promenade and park connectors along Geylang River.

Education

Ideal for families, positioned close to the District 15 / East Coast educational belt. Nearby schools include Dunman High School (within walking distance), Chung Cheng High School (Main), and Broadrick Secondary School.

Investment

First GLS in 30 years with record-breaking developer confidence ($1,455 psf ppr). Surrounded by upcoming HDB BTO projects creating built-in upgrader demand. Beneficiary of the Kallang Alive Masterplan transforming the precinct into Singapore's premier sports and lifestyle hub.
