### 📍 Location & District

* **District:** District 27 (D27) - Located in the Outside Central Region (OCR).
* **Address/Location:** Chencharu Close, situated at the forefront of Yishun.
* **Public Transport:** Highly accessible, located just a 6 to 8-minute walk to Khatib MRT Station (North-South Line).
* **Road Connectivity:** Direct connections to Sembawang Road and Lentor Avenue, linking to the Seletar Expressway (SLE). The upcoming North-South Corridor (from 2027) will shorten commutes to Central Singapore by up to 30 minutes.

### 💰 Land Price & Developer

* **Land Price (Top Bid):** $1.013 billion, which translates to $980 psf ppr (per square foot per plot ratio).
* **Developer Consortium:** Evia MCS Pte. Ltd., Gamuda (Singapore) Pte. Ltd., and H108 Pte. Ltd.

### 🏢 Development Scale

* **Expected Number of Units:** 875 private residential units.
* **Project Type:** A massive Mixed-Use Commercial & Residential Development.
* **Scale:** It is the second-largest mixed-use project in the North Region, following North Park Residence and Northpoint City.

### ⭐ Unique Selling Points (USPs)

* **Fully Integrated Hub:** The development seamlessly integrates private condominium housing with a brand-new bus interchange, a hawker centre, retail shops, dining establishments, and community spaces all in one place.
* **First-Mover Advantage:** Chencharu is Yishun's newest growth node. The government plans to build 8,000 new 'Standard' HDB flats here by 2040, securing a strong future pool of HDB upgraders for exit strategies.
* **Pent-Up Demand:** This is an incredibly rare supply. The last mixed-use development launch in Yishun was in 2015 (The Wisteria), and the last new private condo launch within 1km of Khatib MRT was way back in 2010 (The Estuary).
* **Extensive Neighborhood Amenities:** In close proximity to Northpoint City, Yishun Stadium, Khoo Teck Puat Hospital, and HomeTeam NS Hub.
* **Active Mobility & Nature:** Designed with extensive walking and cycling paths linking directly to neighboring towns like Sembawang, the North-South Corridor, and recreational nodes like Lower Pierce Reservoir.
* **Family-Friendly:** Positioned in an area with an exceptional variety of educational offerings, making it highly attractive for families with school-going children.
